# Design notes — snaplead-lead-detail.pptx

Reference source: `snaplead-lead-detail.pptx`.

## Direction

- Interface B2B dense, sobre, utilitaire.
- Fond principal clair cassé, cartes bleu nuit, texte très contrasté.
- Accent chaud brique pour les CTA et marqueurs importants.
- Vert réservé aux scores/signaux positifs.
- Données commerciales présentées en blocs compacts, façon fiche opérationnelle.

## Palette observed

- Navy: `#141C2D`
- Muted slate: `#4A5365`
- Secondary text: `#7A8294`
- Light background: `#F6F4EF`
- Cream surface/text: `#F1EDE4`
- Accent brick: `#C2542D`
- Success green: `#1F7A52`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

## UI Patterns

- Lead detail starts with a strong identity header: breadcrumb, capture context, status, company name, NAF/activity.
- Score card is prominent, numeric `/100`, with detailed matching signals below.
- Commercial actions are first-class: angle, call script, email, copy buttons.
- Similar companies appear as ranked cards with order number, distance, business identity, key fields, and one short sales hook.
- Mobile views split the lead detail into focused screens:
  - Overview
  - Actions commerciales
  - Entreprises similaires
- Buttons use concise action labels: `Copier`, `Exporter CSV`, `Archiver`, `Marquer comme contacté`.

## Copy Tone

- Direct, field-sales oriented, no marketing fluff.
- French business wording.
- Terrain context is explicit: photo/capture location, date, vehicle/sign sighting.
- Sales copy should sound practical and conversational, not automated.
